Chocolate Cake

Here is the fast and easy version of the classic Chocolate Cake recipe. Enjoy!

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total time
Easy 10 Mins 25 Mins 35 Mins

Ingedients

Black Chocolate: 200 gms

Sugar: 150gms

Butter: 200 gms

Flour: 80 gms

Instructions

1

2

3
